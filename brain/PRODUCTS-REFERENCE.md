# ProfitPulse Services Reference

Upload this file to the ProfitPulse Daily Blog Claude Project as a knowledge file. It is the source of truth for which services to reference in blog posts and which URL to link to. Pricing is included for internal reference only; never quote prices in blog body content. Direct readers to the relevant page where they will find current pricing.

When weaving a service into a post, refer to it by its name (e.g. "13-Week Cash Flow Build", not "C1"). Use the URL provided for the internal link, with `target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"` applied.

## Category A: Diagnostic & Analytical Engagements

**Profit Pulse Check** — Focused review of the last 12 months of financials that pinpoints where margin is leaking and ranks the three highest-value fixes by dollar impact. Output is a one-page action list with a quantified target.
Themes: profit erosion, margin leak, financial health, starting points for new clients
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Profit & Cash Diagnostic** — Profit leak analysis combined with a 13-week cash flow forecast. Identifies both margin recovery and timing risk in one report.
Themes: cash flow stress, profit erosion, working capital
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Financial Health Check (Quarterly)** — Structured quarterly review against the business plan, with variance analysis, three forward-looking risks and three opportunities. Keeps the business decision-ready every 90 days.
Themes: discipline, rhythm, board readiness, planning
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Strategic Growth Diagnostic** — Six-week engagement that maps revenue, capacity and margin headroom, then produces a 12-month growth plan with funding and capital allocation steps spelled out.
Themes: growth strategy, scaling, capital allocation
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Capital Allocation Review** — Independent review of where capital is deployed (people, stock, fit-out, marketing, capex) against return generated. Produces a re-deployment recommendation with expected ROI on each move.
Themes: capital efficiency, ROI, investment decisions, capex
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Full Commercial Audit** — The most comprehensive standalone engagement: complete review of P&L, balance sheet, working capital, capex, pricing, customer profitability and strategic positioning. Delivers a board-grade report.
Themes: comprehensive review, board readiness, pre-transaction prep
Link: https://profit-pulse.com.au/financial-analysis

**Supplier & Payables Optimisation** — Renegotiation playbook for the top 20 suppliers covering payment terms, early-pay discounts and consolidation opportunities.

**Banking & Facility Review** — Independent review of existing bank facilities, covenants and pricing, with a refinance recommendation. Designed to pay back through interest savings within the first year.

**Treasury & FX Setup** — For businesses with overseas suppliers or customers. Establishes hedging policy, FX provider review and a monthly treasury rhythm.
